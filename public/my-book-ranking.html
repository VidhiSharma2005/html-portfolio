Absolute Greatest Books Of All Time

My Top Book Recs of ALL Time

Monster Trilogy

-By Rina Kent

This book was an abasolute joy to read and not just because of my personal bias toward gender bender books and movies. I am a sucker for that shit and I just can't get enough of it. This book was an absolute treat with well developed character with touching backstories and a compelling story. There was tangible and sweet, delicious tension and the way that their relationaship developed felt natural, well paced and satisfying. With an appropriate amount of betrayals, sweet confessions and grovelling, I am in love.
Rating: 10/10
